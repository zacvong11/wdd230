Angier, NC Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The site name for this Chamber of Commerce website is "Angier Chamber of Commerce". This name was chosen because it clearly represents the town of Angier, North Carolina, and its goal of fostering business growth and community involvement in the area. The name reflects the organization's mission to connect local businesses, promote economic development, and provide resources for the community.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is twofold:

  • To promote and support local businesses in Angier by providing a platform for business listings, events, and networking opportunities.
  • To attract tourists, new residents, and businesses to Angier by showcasing the town's attractions, history, and community offerings, while advocating for economic development in the area.

Scenarios

  • What local events are being held this month that foster business-to-business networking?
  • How can I become a member of the Angier Chamber of Commerce, and what are the membership benefits?
  • What is the current population of Angier, NC, and how has it changed in the last few years?

Color Schema

The color scheme for this site includes the following:

  • Primary Color: #005A71 (Deep Teal) - Used for headings, navigation bar, and footer background. This color conveys professionalism and stability, aligning with the chamber's business-oriented mission.
  • Secondary Color: #F4A261 (Warm Amber) - Used for call-to-action buttons, hover states, and highlights. This color adds a warm, inviting feel to the site, encouraging engagement with local businesses and community members.

Typography

The following fonts will be used on the website:

  • Heading Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif - Used for all headings (H1, H2, etc.). This modern, clean font is professional and easy to read.
  • Body Font: 'Lora', serif - Used for paragraphs and general content. Lora provides a balance of readability and elegance, making it suitable for longer text blocks.

Wireframe

Mobile View: The mobile version will feature a stacked layout with the header at the top, followed by the navigation bar, main content (highlighting local businesses and upcoming events), and a footer with contact details.

Desktop View: The desktop version will have a top navigation bar, a large hero image showcasing Angier's attractions, followed by sections for the business directory, upcoming events, and membership information, with a footer at the bottom for contact and social links.
